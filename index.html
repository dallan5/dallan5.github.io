Background

About

CG Technical Director at Important Looking Pirates (Stockholm, 2017–2025). Lighting and lookdev origin; pipeline and creature TD by trade.

Artist at heart who loves technology. The work I'm proudest of sits at the boundary — reducing friction so the people I work with can focus on craft, not workflow.

At UC Irvine studying ML to prepare for the next generation of creative tools. My belief: ML's place in production right now is supporting the artist feedback loop, not replacing creative decisions.
